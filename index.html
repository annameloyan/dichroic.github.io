Cyber Arts Camp

Learn cutting edge technology and sustainability from the leading minds in their fields.

Join us on an adventure across the universe. The martians are in the dark and need YOU to build a light and illuminate the night sky. Beings from across the universe will teleport in along the way to teach YOU the skills necessary to build your own light sculpture.

Lessons

  • Learn

    We'll take you on a journey to design, construct, and illuminate your own sculpture. No prior coding experience necessary. Find all materials included in your space kit.

  • Arduino

    Become the master of your universe with an Arduino.

  • LEDs

    Go from 0 to 255 with RGB. Learn how to address individual LEDs, additive versus subtractive mediums, and explore how rainbows are created through light refraction.

  • Solar Power

    Learn to harness the power of sun through our solar power instructions.

  • Art

    Cyber Arts Camp coaches are artists working at the intersection of technology and art. Students will engage with material through artistic problem solving.

  • Sculpture

    Lets travel to the third dimension with the help of Buckminster Fuller while exploring geometric and architectural principles.

  • Modularity

    Component parts, a whole comprised of indivudal pieces, reconfiguration and variation

  • Sustainability

    Turn trash into treasure.

  • Create

    Synthesize these concepts into a unique light sculpture

FAQ

  • When does the camp run?

    Cyber Arts Camp starts June 23rd and will run for two weeks. 10 total classes.

  • What is a day in cyber arts camp like?

    We start the day with an entertaining and educational video on the topic of the day. We then show detailed instructions on how to build and work on the subject of the day. Total time is about 2 to 5 hours a day.

  • Will there be coaches or support?

    Yes, we have Martian coaches that will help along the way during studio hours and through online chat.

  • What will we be building?

    At the end of the semester, each student will have designed, built, soldered, and programmed their own unique light sculpture.

  • What results can a student expect?

    Students will walk away with a basic understanding of physical computing, electronics, and a feeling of empowerment to create.

  • Are materials provided?

    Yes, we will be sending a Martian kit that will have all the materials needed including LED lights, Arduino, Soldering Kit, Building Materials, a $100 dollar value!

  • Is supervision advised?

    Yes, during our soldering and solar power tutorials (2 days) adult supervision is advised as we'll be working with a soldering iron.

  • Will computers be provided?

    No, in order to enjoy the camp a computer or laptop with internet access is required.
